about

MyKeja is a web app that unifies the landlord, caretaker -if any- and the tenants. This is achieved through a well crafted database that stores information about the clients and is easily accessible to the parties involved.

features

manage apartments

This feature is for landlords who have multiple apartments. They will be able to create new apartments and add their tenants and caretakers

hakiba pay

Haba na haba hujaza kibaba. This feature is designed to track payment information especially in small amounts. It will be accessible to all parties and will show the payment progress of tenants.
