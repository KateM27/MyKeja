about

MyKeja is a web app that unifies the landlord, caretaker -if any- and the tenants. this is archived through a well crafted database that stores information about the clients and is easily accessible to the parties involved.

features

manage apartments

this is created for landlords who have more that one apartments they are managing. they will be able to create new apartments and add their tenants and caretakers

hakiba pay

haba na haba hujaza kibaba. this is feature is designed to track payment information. especially in small amount. it will be accessible to all parties and will show payment progress.
